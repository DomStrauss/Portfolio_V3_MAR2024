Excel VBA and Dashboards

Empowering Clinical Research with Excel VBA

Excel serves as a trustworthy data storage, manipulation, and basic analysis tool. Excel dashboards transform raw data into actionable insights. Interactive charts , slicers, pivot tables, and filters can help visualize trial progress, safety metrics, recruitment rates, and patient demographics. Excel Dashboards is a tool that can help make fast decisions eith accurate data.

Excel VBA can be useful in the CRO environment for several areas. VBA scripts validate data integrity, flag inconsistencies, and perform quality checks and can also help ensure compliance with regulatory standards (ICH-GCP, FDA) through automated checks. Excel macros (powered by VBA) can automate mundane tasks: data cleaning, formatting, and report generation. Macros can auto-populate case report forms (CRFs) or extract specific data subsets effortlessly. VBA can also facilitate seamless data exchange by exporting data to statistical software (SAS, R) or integrate data with electronic data capture (EDC) systems. Real-time data feeds are possible from EDC platforms directly into Excel.

Global species diversity and distribution of the psychedelic fungal genus Panaeolus.

Heliyon 2023

doi.org/10.1016/j.heliyon.2023.e16338

The aim of this review is to summarise current scientific data and knowledge on the Panaeolus species biodiversity, geographical distribution, extent of sequence data for identification purposes, and the psychedelic potential of species, based on published results. The review revealed where species are mostly known from, while also indicating areas seriously lacking such biodiversity data. A significant degree of study across the world is still needed to confirm which of these species are truly psychedelic and exactly what compounds they can produce.
